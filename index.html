Who I am

I'm a 16-year-old developer from Canada who ships real, production systems: web platforms, Minecraft plugins, payment infrastructure, and open-source security hardware. By day I'm a Future Tech Intern at Nokia; the rest of the time I build Mythofy Inc., a company I founded.
